Our Services

Daily Contributions

Users can contribute small amounts daily to grow their savings or support community goals over time.

Group Savings

Allow users to create or join contribution groups where members contribute regularly and take turns receiving payouts.

User Dashboard

A personalized dashboard where users can track contributions, balances, payouts, and activity history.

Secure Payments Integration

Enable safe deposits and withdrawals using trusted payment gateways like bank transfer, cards, or mobile payments.

Contribution Plans

Users can choose flexible plans (daily, weekly, monthly) depending on their financial goals.

Automated Reminders

Send notifications or emails reminding users to make their contributions on time.

Withdrawal & Payout System

Users can request withdrawals or receive scheduled payouts directly to their bank accounts.

Community Groups

Users can create private or public groups for friends, families, or coworkers to contribute together.
